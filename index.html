What does the free AWS assessment include?

A full review of your AWS environment covering IAM and access controls, security group and network posture, backup setup, spend analysis with rightsizing recommendations, Reserved Instance and Savings Plan opportunities, and an architecture review against AWS Well-Architected best practices. You receive a written report with prioritised findings and estimated savings, completely free with zero obligation.

What does "no results, no fee" mean in practice?

We agree on measurable targets upfront, typically cost savings, security improvements, or deployment frequency gains. If we do not hit those targets, you do not pay for the implementation work. We are confident in our results, which is why we put our fees on the line.

Do you need access to our AWS account?

For the assessment we use read-only IAM access. We never require write permissions at this stage. We provide an exact policy document with minimum permissions needed. All access is auditable, time-limited, and revocable instantly. We sign an NDA before any access is granted.

How long does a typical engagement take?

The free assessment is completed and reported within 3 to 5 working days. Cost optimisation engagements show results in 2 to 4 weeks. Full migrations or DevOps transformations are scoped in phases with clear milestones. You will have a detailed timeline before any work begins.

What size businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes across the world, from fast-growing startups to established enterprises. Our model is built for organisations that want senior expertise and real accountability, without the overhead of a large consultancy.

What is an AWS Golden Jacket holder?

The AWS Golden Jacket is the highest community recognition AWS grants, awarded to individuals with exceptional multi-year contributions to the AWS community. Fewer than 100 people hold it globally. It cannot be purchased or applied for, and it represents the deepest level of AWS technical expertise and community trust.

What does Cyber Essentials readiness mean?

Cyber Essentials is a UK government-backed certification required by many public sector contracts and supply chains. We assess your AWS environment against its five controls and implement any remediation needed, covering firewalls, access control, secure configuration, malware protection, and patch management.
