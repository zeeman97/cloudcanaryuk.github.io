Recovery plans that actually work.

Existing strategy assessment

We start by reviewing what you already have in place, your backup coverage, retention policies, storage costs, and any gaps in your current setup. Most organisations are surprised to find critical workloads that are either unprotected or backed up inconsistently.

⚙️
Automated backup implementation

We implement AWS Backup policies that run automatically, cover all relevant services, and store data in the right locations with appropriate retention windows. No more manual processes or forgotten snapshots.

📋
RTO and RPO definition

Recovery Time Objective and Recovery Point Objective are not just theoretical terms. We work with your team to define what they actually mean for each workload, then build a DR architecture that can genuinely meet those targets.

🧪
Recovery simulation and testing

A backup plan you have never tested is a plan you cannot rely on. We simulate real failure scenarios, run full recovery exercises, and document the results so you know exactly how your environment responds under pressure.

📖
Runbooks and documentation

When something goes wrong, your team needs to move fast. We produce clear, step-by-step runbooks for each recovery scenario so anyone on the team can execute a recovery without needing to improvise under pressure.
